How It Works
  1. Fetch Articles: Click "Fetch Articles from RSS Feeds" to collect new articles from all configured news sources. The system will:
    • Parse RSS feeds from Financial Times, NYT, WSJ, Bloomberg, etc.
    • Automatically classify articles by topic and sentiment
    • Add new articles to the database with outcome="NEW"
    • Skip duplicates (based on title + source + published date)
  2. Review Articles: Go to the Articles page to review new articles, assign them to story groups, and mark them as processed or rejected.
  3. Process: Articles marked as "processed" are ready for the next stage.
Integrated & Fast: The aggregator is now fully integrated into Django. No separate processes or dual databases!
