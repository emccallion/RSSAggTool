Typical Workflow
  1. Run Aggregator: Click "Run Aggregator" to fetch new articles from RSS feeds. This updates the news.db database.
  2. Sync Articles: Click "Sync Articles" to import new articles into the preprocessing database. Only new articles (not already in preprocessing.db) will be added.
  3. Review Articles: Go to the Articles page to review, categorize, and process articles.
  4. Repeat: Run this workflow regularly to keep your preprocessing database up to date.
Tip: Articles are matched on (title, source, published date) to avoid duplicates. The sync process is safe to run multiple times.
